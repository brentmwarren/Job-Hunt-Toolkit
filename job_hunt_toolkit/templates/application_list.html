Finding a job in the Bay Area is nothing less than a daunting task. This app is designed to help anyone who wants help getting started. We'll be walking through guidelines for resume/linkedin tailoring, job hunting stategies, applying to the jobs themselves, and where to go from there. Let's get started!

Resume Building tips

You need to have an up-to-date resume to submit that is free of typos

list your work/personal experience

don’t be afraid of your job history. Everyones life is different, you’ve got a story to tell

just a few bullet boints per job. It doesn't need to be very wordy as long as a recruiter can get an accurate idea of your experience. Be concise and leave room to talk. Resumes don’t need to be word-for-word

your personal contact information, phone number and email, need to be listed

stick to simple designs that arent too cluddered and dont over do color. I personally use some blue in my resume. Make sure to give the file a relevant name

don’t be afraid to have multiple versions of your resume that are slightly different and tailored towards a company/job you really want
